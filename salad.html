Waldorf Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 large apples, cored and chopped
  • 1 cup celery, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup walnuts, chopped
  • 1/2 cup seedless grapes, halved
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup raisins (optional)

Method

  1. In a large bowl, combine the apples, celery, walnuts, and grapes.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise and lemon juice. Season with salt and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss until all the ingredients are well coated.
  4. If using raisins, add them to the salad and mix well.
  5. Serve chilled.
