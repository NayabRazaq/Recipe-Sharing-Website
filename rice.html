Rice with Manchurian

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup chopped vegetables (carrot, bell pepper, etc.)
  • 200g chicken, cubed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup tomato ketchup
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil

Method

  1. In a pan, heat the oil and saut√© the onions and garlic until golden brown.
  2. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Add the chopped vegetables and cook until tender.
  4. Mix the soy sauce, tomato ketchup, and cornflour in a bowl.
  5. Pour the sauce mixture into the pan and stir until thickened.
  6. Serve the Manchurian with cooked rice and enjoy!
