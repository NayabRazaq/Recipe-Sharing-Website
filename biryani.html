Delicious Biryani

Ingredients

  • 500g Basmati rice
  • 500g chicken
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • 2 tbsp ghee

Method

  1. Soak the rice for 30 minutes and then boil until 70% cooked.
  2. In a separate pot, heat ghee and fry onions until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger garlic paste, tomatoes, and chicken. Cook until the chicken is tender.
  4. Mix in yogurt, garam masala, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook for a few more minutes.
  5. Layer the rice over the chicken mixture, garnish with coriander and mint leaves.
  6. Cover and cook on low heat until the rice is fully cooked and flavors are combined.
  7. Serve hot with raita or salad.
