Chili Fish

Ingredients

  • 500g fish fillets
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 green chilies, sliced
  • 2 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp red chili sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying

Method

  1. Marinate the fish with soy sauce, vinegar, cornflour, and ginger-garlic paste. Set aside for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and fry the marinated fish until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, saut√© onions, bell pepper, and green chilies until they soften.
  4. Add tomato ketchup and red chili sauce to the vegetables. Stir well.
  5. Add the fried fish to the pan and mix until the fish is coated with the sauce.
  6. Cook for another 5 minutes and serve hot.
