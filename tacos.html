Baked Tacos

Ingredients

  • 10 taco shells
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 1 can black beans
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • Sour cream (optional)
  • Lettuce and tomato for topping

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a skillet, cook the ground beef until browned. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add the taco seasoning and black beans to the beef. Stir well.
  4. Place taco shells in a baking dish. Fill each shell with the beef mixture.
  5. Top with shredded cheese and bake for 10 minutes or until the cheese is melted.
  6. Serve with salsa, onions, lettuce, tomato, and sour cream if desired.
